Bio!

I was born and raised in a Kurdish family in Boukan, a small city in Iran's northwest. My childhood was full of beautiful times with my cousins and friends, playing soccer and video games almost all the time.

Throughout my school years, I tied to be the top student, and I was. I was graduated from Farabi high school in 2015 and took the national university entrance exam.

I started studying computer software engineering at Urmia University. This period was life-changing for me as I got familiar with the world of computer technology, learned lessons that changed my vision for life, and made incredible friends. In the summer of 2019, I graduated from university and also took Iran's higher education exam.

Now I'm studying Master's at K.N.Toosi university of technology, and I hope to find the opportunity to enhance my research abilities by working with great scientists in the world.
